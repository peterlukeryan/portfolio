Projects

  • QuickMaths- iOs (2022)

    What's 46 squared? 64 X 83? What if I told you that you could calculate these in under five seconds, all in your head?

    We've been taught to multiply with pencil and paper. Makes sense. This style, unsurprisingly, saves paper. But QuickMaths teaches you a different way to do things.

    "Left to Right" multiplication is a powerful technique used by the world's best mental calculators.

    QuickMaths demonstrates this technique visually, in a fun, intuitive way-- perfect for youngsters and adults alike. Gotten pretty good? Try your skills out in the arena...

  • BlurbIt- Python with Flask (2022)

    Four youngsters go out into the woods one weekend for some fun.

    Things start out great, everyone's laughing, it's a blast. Then, out of nowehere, they begin to hear a ghostly screech from the woods.

    At first it's no big deal, but soon enough, they find out that they're being terrorized by a demonic spirit, hellbent on turning the trip of a lifetime into a nightmare.

    Sound spooky? BlurbIt thinks so.
    BlurbIt is a web application that takes in blurbs, like the creepy one above, and predicts the genre you're trying to describe-- using a custom Logistic Regression Model and data processed from scratch-- no pre-trained models whatsoever.

    A passion project exploring SciKit learn and Python's Flask module, BlurbIt is a pretty cool example of the power of machine learning. Check it out!

  • Wonder- iOs (2017-2018)

    Travel. Few things ignite the imagination the same way-- new sights, new sounds, new experience. The trouble with travel? Ignorance. The Parthenon and a congress building aren't that different if you don't know anything about ancient Greece. Wonder was designed to bridge this gap.

    A simple, easy to use camera app that not only identifies landmarks, but displays interesting information from the site's Wikipedia page,all in real time. Includes a "Did you know?" button, which pulls a random fact that you can annoy your friends with at your next cocktail party.
